COVID Shield

Combined with the Google & Apple Exposure Notification API, this is the lowest-cost, highest-speed course of action to getting Canadians safely back to work.

Notifications when they matter

Our goal is to help Canadians stay safe as possible while the economy recovers and people return to work. We have done this by creating an app that uses exposure notifications (also known as ‘digital contact tracing’) to notify Canadians of potential exposure to individuals with positive COVID-19 test results.
