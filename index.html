COVID Shield

Combined with the Apple & Google Exposure Notification API, this is the most privacy-preserving course of action to help Canadians get safely back to work.

Source code on GitHub

Notifications when they matter

COVID Shield runs in the background and only notifies you if you've possibly been exposed to someone who has tested positive for COVID-19.

Privacy as a top priority

COVID Shield has been designed with user privacy as the top priority. We must strike a balance between opening the economy and preserving personal privacy, and COVID Shield takes a privacy-first approach. The COVID Shield app, its backend server, and the portal are all designed to make it as difficult as possible to personally identify users or link them to their test results. To learn more about how we’ve approached privacy, read our privacy policy.
